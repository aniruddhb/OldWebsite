ColorIO

An application that allows users to randomly generate FlatUI colors, save these colors to their "color palettes", and email these FlatUI colors to themselves (to prevent them from forgetting about their beautiful colors palettes). The aim of this app was to help designers for websites create color palettes that are appealing to the eyes, instead of poorly designed UI's and UX's.

Project Details

  • git.io/v4jZX

  • November 2015

  • ButtonViews, Facebook SDK and API Integration, Programmatic Assignment of TextView and ButtonView text, Using EMAIL integration in application, Saving Colors to Palette using ArrayList Structures, FlatUI and well-designed UX's
