Sliding Window (LZ77) Compression Methods

The LZ77, or Sliding Window, method of compression was designed such that pointers to characters seen in the past are substituted in for future instances of the seen character sequences. I implemented a rudimentary, but efficient and quick, algorithm that effectively compressed large text files (Bible, Hamlet, etc.) into smaller outputs. As a "freebie", I also implemented a decompression algorithm as well (you're welcome!)
